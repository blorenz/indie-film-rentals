About Us

Producers across the country trust Ohio HD Video when network quality crews and production gear are needed throughout Ohio and the Midwest. With over 20 years in the business, we know your expectations are high, and we work to exceed them everyday.

Our DPs have years of real-world experience with a wide range of cameras and formats. Whether you choose to shoot an Arri, Sony, Panasonic, or Canon, Ohio HD VIdeo knows cameras and how to get the most out of each one.

Our rental gear division is strong with over 35 camera packages in-house, a large lens inventory, and resources that stretch across the country. If we don't have it, we can get it.

Crew Bios

Scott Handel

At the heart of Ohio HD Video is 20 year veteran Directory of Photography, Scott Handel. Scott brings a combination of lighting skills and an eye for framing detail to a wide range of diverse projects. From Network-level ENG, Magazine Shows, Reality TV, Commercial Production, Corporate Video, Web or Satellite Media Tours, Scott and his crews always deliver the images that keep producers coming back year after year. Recent clients include, Discovery Channel, ABC, FOX News, FOX Sports, Big Ten Network, ESPN, Chase Bank, Purina, Gatlinburg Tourism, Victoria's Secret and more.

Casey Hunter

Since he was a child Casey was fascinated with how things worked. He was always tearing something apart, salvaging parts, and making his own creations. Casey came to Ohio HD Video with Bachelor's of Science in Computer Information Systems from DeVry University and a keen interest in filmmaking. By working hands-on with the equipment at Ohio HD Video, as well as on live sets, Casey has become valued member of the production community as "the guy with all the answers." If you find yourself with Casey on set working as an AC, Camera Tech or DIT, you know your production is in great hands.

David Jefferies

David has always been involved with some kind of technical and creative industry. Whether in theater, building sets and working in sound and lighting or in world of production honing his craft with an attention to framing and shaping light. While attending Ohio University David continued to work as a production and camera assistant in the film industry in Cleveland. At Ohio HD Video David is working with cutting edge of camera technology to sculpt his photographic eye and is proven leader on set.

John Thorne

John tackles each project with a positive, team-oriented attitude and has applied his diverse skill set in a variety of industries including marketing, customer relations management and live television production. John earned a degree from Hocking College in Audio/Video Production and has shot and edited projects for a range of clients including Walmart, Macy's, MSN and McDonald's.
