About Ohio HD Video

Ohio HD Video has steadily grown through the years to provide producers with top notch ENG & EFP crews in Ohio and the largest camera inventory in the state. We work everyday to deliver dynamic award winning images from Columbus, Cincinnati, Dayton, Cleveland and beyond. Our crews have tackled projects in 49 states and 17 countries.

At the heart of Ohio HD Video is 20 year veteran DP/Cameraman Scott Handel. Scott brings a combination of lighting skills and an eye for framing detail to a wide range of diverse projects. From Network-level ENG, Magazine Shows, Reality TV, Commercial Production, Corporate Video, Web or Satellite Media Tours, Scott and his crews always deliver the images that keep producers coming back year after year.
